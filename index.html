Frequently Asked Questions

Who does Trovixo Digital design services help?

Our affordable design support services are created for small business owners, local service providers, and student freelancers who want to build a professional digital presence.

What is the difference between a Template and a Custom design?

Template posts use pre-made layout structures styled with your brand colors and text. Custom designs are engineered completely from scratch tailored uniquely for that specific piece of content.

How long does it take to get custom carousel designs?

Our custom carousel designs and multi-slide post templates are delivered within 2 to 5 days, including two full rounds of edits to match your exact business branding.

What do your affordable brand audit services include?

Every basic brand audit includes written feedback, actionable profile layout suggestions, an Instagram bio review, and clear visual improvement steps to help you build instant customer trust.

How do I receive my editable Canva files?

Once final payment is made, we send over a direct template access link. You can save this link directly to your own free or pro Canva account to make future edits.

Can I request extra revisions if needed?

Yes. Every single project includes 2 rounds of standard edits. If you require further structural shifts after that, minor design maintenance charges can be added.

What details do I need to provide to start?

Simply hit the "Start Project" button and fill out our client form. Having your text ready, brand colors, and any specific layout images handy helps our delivery timelines immensely.
